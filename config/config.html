Bower

Use your project's local Bower dependency. This means you have `bower` in the `devDependencies` section of your package.json.

Optional. Usually set if your bower.json is located deeper down in the folder structure of your repository, or if there are multiple projects in this one repository. A valid value is something like "myproject/", notice the lack of a slash in the beginning.

Caching

Strict
Loose
None

Strict cache your modules according to an md5 hash of the bower.json. Any change will cause a fresh `bower install`, w/o the cache.

Loose cache everything, and run `bower update` and `bower prune` after restoring the cache.
